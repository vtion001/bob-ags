        iPhone       Cluely turns your phone into an AI notetaker that transcribes, gives real-time assistance, and takes perfect notes for you — whether you're in person or on a call.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Tap Start Recording
2. Cluely records and transcribes in real-time
3. Ask AI for assistance during the conversation
4. Get detailed notes seconds after the recording ends
